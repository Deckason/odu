Security Policy

At Odu, we approach security as both a technical requirement and a collective responsibility. Our policies reflect our commitment to protecting our community while maintaining user privacy and operational transparency.

▲

Foundational Practices

We maintain security through:

  • • End-to-end encrypted communications. SSL/TLS, signed for sensitive matters
  • • Minimal data retention: Collecting only what is essential
  • • Zero-trust architecture: Assuming breach and verifying access
  • • Regular security audit: Third-party vulnerability

Penetration tests with awareness

▲

Digital Safeguards

For all users

  • • Use strong passphrases + two factor authentication
  • • Verify contacts through official channels
  • • Enable security notifications and alerts
  • • Consult encrypted tools for sensitive discussions

Our infrastructure

  • • Enterprise-grade firewalls + intrusion detection
  • • Secure coding practices for all development
  • • Automated dependency scanning and patches
  • • Open source solutions where verifiable
▲

Organizational Security

We stand on three legs: Sensitive information is compartmentalized:

  • • Used to tickets issues: Sensitive information is compartmentalized
  • • Continuous training: Regular OPSEC education for members
  • • Physical security: Secure facilities with access controls
  • • Incident protocols: User response plans for breaches
▲

Community Protection

History teaches that strong networks:

  • • Share security knowledge organically
  • • Monitor threats to collective infrastructure
  • • Support members facing targeting
  • • Adapt lessons from global movements
▲

Responsible Disclosure

Found a vulnerability?

  • • Email security@odu.com with details
  • • Allow 7 - business days for response
  • • Never exploit or publicly disclose before resolution
▲

Our Commitment

We pledge to:

  • • Regularly Update these protocols
  • • Share updates with members
  • • Maintain continuous infrastructure security practices
  • • Honor the digital sovereignty of all members
